# DFORTH — Impeccable Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
Primary visitor is **enterprise procurement / vendor-evaluation teams** vetting DFORTH
(brand of Dexio LabX Pvt. Ltd.) as an outsourcing & dedicated-tech-talent partner.
They read carefully, compare against competitors, and need to feel they're dealing
with a serious engineering organization. Decision context: desktop, daytime, due-diligence
mindset. They care about credibility, process maturity, scale, and proof — not hype.

### Brand Personality
**Confident · technical · precise.** Engineering-led and restrained. Speaks like a team
that ships, not a team that markets. Emotional goal: *trust through evident craft.* The
interface itself should be the proof of competence.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Editorial light.** Magazine-grade typography, strong asymmetric grids, generous
whitespace, clear hierarchy. Light theme (warm-cool paper, deep ink). One sharp accent
used sparingly. Distinctive and modern without being loud.

- **References (feel):** editorial/print layout systems, serious engineering tool docs,
  a well-set annual report.
- **Anti-references (must NOT look like):** gradient text, gradient buttons, decorative
  blobs, glassmorphism, neon-on-dark, purple→blue AI gradients, identical icon-card grids,
  centered-everything templates. (The current site uses several of these — they are being removed.)

### Design Principles
1. **Type carries the design.** A grotesque display (authority/technical) paired with a
   refined serif body (editorial/trust). Hierarchy through scale + weight contrast, not color.
2. **Whitespace is structure.** Asymmetric, left-aligned, intentionally broken grids.
   Not everything is a card; nothing is a card-in-a-card.
3. **One accent, earned.** ~10% visual weight max. Color signals action and emphasis only.
4. **Evidence over adjectives.** Show process, numbers, and specifics; let restraint imply rigor.
5. **No AI tells.** If it would read as "an AI made this," rebuild the element differently.

### Type & Color (canonical tokens)
- Display: **Archivo** (expanded width, 700–900) — confident, technical headlines.
- Body: **Spectral** (400/500) — editorial, trustworthy reading.
- Palette: OKLCH. Brand hue is the existing logo violet (#9370DB ≈ 300°), rendered as a
  deep editorial plum-ink (NOT a gradient). Neutrals tinted toward 300°. The logo magenta
  (#EA4EE3 ≈ 350°) is the single rare sharp accent.
- New design system lives in `assets/css/dforth.css`; homepage (`index.html`) is the flagship.
  Other pages migrate to this system afterward.
